Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce
    1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    2. Add marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix Cheese Filling
    1. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Mix well.
  5. Assemble Lasagna
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    2. Layer 4 noodles over the sauce, followed by half of the cheese mixture, and then another layer of meat sauce.
    3. Repeat the layers, ending with noodles topped with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve warm.

Enjoy Your Lasagna

This hearty lasagna recipe is perfect for family dinners and gatherings.
